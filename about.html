About Burlington Indivisible

We are a Burlington, MA based grassroots organization focused on restraining ICE, getting out the vote, and advocating for downstream ballot candidates that will run fair elections. Our mission is to empower people to rein in ICE and create lasting change for a vibrant, just democracy.

We Make It Easier to Get Involved

We make it easier to get involved. Our website curates the best organizations and resources so you can quickly find meaningful ways to act. Browse by resource or filter actions by cause, action type, frequency and mode (in-person, on your own, virtual).

We highlight established local groups that:

  • Meet regularly
  • Make participation accessible
  • Provide clear information
  • Have a track record of effective action

1000 District Ave

We are one of several groups that protest the cruel and illegal actions at the ICE detention center on 1000 District Ave, Burlington, MA. Protests are on Monday–Saturday. Burlington Indivisible protests on Monday and Tuesday.

Burlington Indivisible

Burlington Indivisible is a grassroots force dedicated to local action and the defense of liberal policies. We currently focus on getting out the vote and protesting the ICE detention center.

Take Action With Us

We need your voice and your energy to fuel our advocacy. Whether you have five minutes for a phone call or an afternoon for a protest, there is a place for you here.

  • Lobby for Change: Join our coordinated email, calling, and letter-writing campaigns to hold leadership accountable.
  • Protest outside the ICE detention center and hold signs on Burlington Mall Road. Join us every Monday and Tuesdsay.
  • Get Creative: Help us design flyers and craft signs for our next demonstration.
  • Lead the Way: Organize events for the issues you are passionate about and help shape our local impact. We welcome organizers ready to champion other liberal policies and community initiatives.
