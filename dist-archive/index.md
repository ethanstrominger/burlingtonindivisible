# Join Burlington Indivisible

Burlington Indivisible is a grassroots force dedicated to local action and the defense of liberal policies.   We currently focus on getting out the vote and protesting the ICE detention center,

### Take Action With Us
We need your voice and your energy to fuel our advocacy. Whether you have five minutes for a phone call or an afternoon for a protest, there is a place for you here.

* **Lobby for Change:** Join our coordinated email, calling, and letter-writing campaigns to hold leadership accountable.
* **Protest outside the ICE detention center and hold signs on Burlington Mall Road**.  Join us every Monday and Tuesdsay.
* **Get Creative:** Help us design flyers and craft signs for our next demonstration.
* **Lead the Way:** **Organize events for the issues you are passionate about** and help shape our local impact. We welcome organizers ready to champion other liberal policies and community initiatives.


### Upcoming Events
We are launching **monthly lunch and evening gatherings** focused on immigrant rights and voter mobilization. These events are the perfect opportunity to connect with like-minded neighbors and take a stand.
