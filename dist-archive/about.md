## About Burlington Indivisible

We are a Burlington, MA based grassroots organization focused on restraining ICE, getting out the vote, and advocating for downstream ballot candidates that will run fair elections. Our mission is to empower people to rein in ICE and create lasting change for a vibrant, just democracy.

## We Make It Easier to Get Involved

We make it easier to get involved. Our website curates the best organizations and resources so you can quickly find meaningful ways to act. Browse by [resource](resources.html) or [filter actions](actions.html) by cause, action type, frequency and mode (in-person, on your own, virtual).

We highlight established local groups that:

- Meet regularly
- Make participation accessible
- Provide clear information
- Have a track record of effective action

## 1000 District Ave

We are one of several groups that [protest](/protests1000districtave.html) the cruel and illegal actions at the ICE detention center on 1000 District Ave, Burlington, MA. Protests are on Monday–Saturday. Burlington Indivisible protests on Monday and Tuesday.
