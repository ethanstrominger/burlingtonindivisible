Massachusetts U.S. Senators: Ed Markey and Elizabeth Warren.

Demand Oversight Visits to the Burlington ICE Facility

Call your U.S. Senator and U.S. Representative separately using the scripts below. When Congress is in recess, members are back in Massachusetts — the ideal time for them to visit.

Script for Your U.S. Senator (Markey or Warren)

The Senate has 9 recess periods this year. Ask your senator to commit to an unannounced visit at every break.

"Hi, my name is [your name] and I'm a constituent from [your city/town]. I'm calling to urge Senator [Markey/Warren] to conduct unannounced oversight visits to the ICE detention facility at 1000 District Ave. in Burlington. The Senate has 9 recess periods this year — these are exactly when senators can be on the ground in Massachusetts. Will Senator [Markey/Warren] commit to making an unannounced visit during each Senate recess? If not all nine, how many can the Senator commit to? And can I get a callback when the first visit is scheduled? Make it on a Wed, Thu, or Sat between 11AM and 1PM when protestors will be at the facility. If you are not admitted, come use our public address system to denounce the 72-hour rule. When should I call the senator to find out his answer as to how many he plans on conducting?"

Tip: Push for a number. "Will you commit to all 9? If not, how many?" gets a more concrete answer than a yes/no question.

Script for Your U.S. Representative

The House has multiple recess periods as well. Ask your representative to commit to visiting during their breaks.

"Hi, my name is [your name] and I'm a constituent from [your city/town]. I'm calling to urge Representative [Name] to conduct unannounced oversight visits to the ICE detention facility at 1000 District Ave. in Burlington. The House has recess periods throughout the year when members are back in Massachusetts. Will Representative [Name] commit to making an unannounced visit during each House recess? If not every recess, how many can the Representative commit to? And can I get a callback when the first visit is scheduled? Make it on a Wed, Thu, or Sat between 11AM and 1PM when protestors will be at the facility. If you are not admitted, come use our public address system to denounce the 72-hour rule. When should I call the representative to find out his answer as to how many he plans on conducting?"

Tip: Ask for a specific number of visits and a callback when one is scheduled. Don't accept a vague "we'll look into it."
