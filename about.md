## About Burlington Indivisible

We are a Burlington, MA-based grassroots organization dedicated to holding the local ICE facility accountable, exposing the financial ties between Citizens Bank and private prison corporations, and strengthening our democracy through voter engagement.

To drive change, we organize local actions and share updates on community events and campaigns. Check out our [**Events**](events.html) and [**Take Action**](takeaction.html) sections to see how you can get involved.
