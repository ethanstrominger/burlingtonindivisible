Severing the Relationship Between Citizens and Prison Companies

Many people bank with financial institutions that invest in, lend to, or otherwise support private prison companies and detention infrastructure without realizing it. When our money is tied to those systems, we can become financially connected to detention practices that separate families, undermine due process, and harm immigrant communities.

de-icecitizens.bank is a public-pressure and education effort that helps people understand these financial links and take action to break them. The goal is to move our money and civic pressure away from institutions that enable detention and toward accountability, transparency, and human rights.
